FULL DESCRIPTION:
LinkedIn Post Date — LinkedIn UX Toolkit

Tired of LinkedIn showing "2mo ago" instead of real dates? This extension shows you the exact date every post was published — right on the page.

🗓️ EXACT POST DATES
See the real date (e.g., "Jun 18, 2026") right next to every timestamp on LinkedIn. Hover for the full date and time. Choose your preferred format: short, long, or ISO.

🔗 COPY CLEAN URL
One-click copy of a clean post link — no tracking parameters, no junk. Just a clean URL you can share.

📄 AUTO-EXPAND POSTS
No more clicking "see more" on every post. Turn this on and all posts show their full text automatically.

🚫 HIDE PROMOTED POSTS
Remove sponsored posts from your feed. Your feed, your choice.

⚡ HOW IT WORKS
Every LinkedIn post has a unique ID that encodes the exact timestamp. This extension reads that ID and converts it — no servers, no APIs, no data collection. Everything happens locally in your browser.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• Zero data collection
• Zero network requests
• All processing happens locally
• Open source — inspect the code yourself

Perfect for recruiters, content creators, researchers, and anyone who uses LinkedIn daily.
