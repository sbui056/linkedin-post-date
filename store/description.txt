FULL DESCRIPTION:
LinkedIn Post Date — See Exact Dates on Posts

Tired of LinkedIn showing "2mo ago" instead of real dates? This extension shows you the exact date and time every post was published — right next to the timestamp.

🗓️ EXACT POST DATES
See the real date and time (e.g., "6/18, 3:42 PM") next to every timestamp on LinkedIn. Hover for the full date. Choose your preferred format: compact, detailed, or ISO.

Works on:
• Your main feed
• Company and school post pages
• Individual post pages

⚡ HOW IT WORKS
The extension reads the relative timestamps on LinkedIn ("2d", "3mo") and calculates the actual date. No servers, no APIs, no data collection. Everything happens locally in your browser.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• Zero data collection
• Zero network requests
• All processing happens locally
• Open source — inspect the code yourself

Perfect for recruiters, content creators, researchers, and anyone who wants real dates on LinkedIn.
